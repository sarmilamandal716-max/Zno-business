Knowledge Base

Top Business Tips

Actionable, battle-tested advice from the world's most successful entrepreneurs and operators.

01

Build in Public

Share your journey openly — wins, failures, lessons. Transparency builds trust and an organic audience that becomes your first customers.

02

Talk to Customers Daily

The founders who win are obsessed with customer feedback. Schedule 5 customer calls per week. Always. Non-negotiable.

03

Solve One Problem Brilliantly

Breadth is the enemy of depth. The most valuable companies in the world solve one core problem better than anyone else.

04

Price Higher Than You Think

Most founders underprice. High prices signal quality, attract serious buyers, and give you margin to reinvest in growth.

05

Distribution Over Product

A good product with great distribution beats a great product with poor distribution. Build your distribution channel first.

06

Hire Slow, Fire Fast

Every team member shapes your culture. One wrong hire can destroy what took months to build. Be ruthless about fit.

07

Build Systems, Not Just Sales

Chase revenue early, but build systems in parallel. A business that relies only on you isn't an asset — it's a job.

08

Your Network = Net Worth

Invest in relationships before you need them. The best opportunities, hires, and investors come through people who trust you.

09

Measure What Matters

Pick 3 key metrics and obsess over them. Revenue, retention, and referrals tell you everything you need to know.

Growth Playbook

Marketing Strategies That Scale

The exact frameworks used by billion-dollar brands — made accessible for every founder.

01

Content Marketing Flywheel

Build a compounding content engine that attracts, educates, and converts 24/7 without paid ads.

  • Identify 10 core topics your audience searches
  • Create pillar content + 30 supporting pieces
  • Repurpose across every platform in different formats
  • Build email list from content traffic
02

Viral Loop Architecture

Design your product so every new user naturally brings more users. The best growth is baked in, not bolted on.

  • Map the moments users share your product
  • Add incentive layers at each share point
  • Make sharing frictionless and rewarding
  • Measure K-factor and optimize continuously
03

Community-Led Growth

Build a community around a shared identity, not just a product. Communities create moats no competitor can buy.

  • Define the shared mission beyond your product
  • Create exclusive spaces (Discord, Slack, Circle)
  • Empower super-users as community leaders
  • Turn community insights into product features
04

Influencer Micro-Network

Partner with 50 micro-influencers instead of 1 mega-influencer. Higher trust, better ROI, deeper niches.

  • Find influencers with 5K–100K engaged followers
  • Offer equity or rev-share instead of just cash
  • Give creative freedom — it performs better
  • Track attribution with unique codes per creator
05

Email Automation Sequences

Your email list is the only channel you truly own. Build sequences that turn cold leads into raving fans.

  • Lead magnet → welcome sequence (7 emails)
  • Nurture content → case studies → soft sell
  • Behaviour-triggered sequences by action
  • Re-engagement campaign every 90 days
06

SEO Moat Building

Own the first page for 100 high-intent keywords and watch organic revenue compound for years.

  • Programmatic SEO for long-tail opportunities
  • Build topical authority through content clusters
  • Strategic link building through digital PR
  • Technical SEO audits every quarter
